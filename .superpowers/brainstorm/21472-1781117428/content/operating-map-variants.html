C. Live Setup Map

Best for onboarding execution. The map is also a checklist: placeholders become real records as users complete connection tasks.

My current recommendation

Use C as the product shell, with A inside integration nodes and B inside store-operations/routing nodes. That keeps onboarding visual and actionable without forcing every business concept into one giant graph.
